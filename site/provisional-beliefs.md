# Provisional Beliefs

Intuitions and working hypotheses held for stress-testing against future concepts. These aren't claims — they're flagged positions that feel right but haven't been rigorously validated. Each has a checkpoint where it should be revisited.

---

## *c* as a structural "tick rate"

The speed of light is a universal constant that functions as the fundamental rate at which the universe processes — inaccessible to embedded observers as an absolute, but structurally present everywhere.

**Related concepts:** [Speed of Light](knowledge/special-relativity/speed-of-light.md), [Time Dilation](knowledge/special-relativity/time-dilation.md)

**Status:** The "universe ticks at *c*" and "everything moves at *c*" formulations are mathematically identical with identical predictions. Trey's version has a mild explanatory economy advantage: *c* as ceiling is structural/definitional rather than derived.

**Stress-test at:** Background independence (General Relativity)

---

## All observers are fundamentally fixed

The observer doesn't move through the universe — the universe transforms around the observer. Motion is a description of how the universe's state changes relative to a fixed observational frame.

**Related concepts:** [Inertial Frames](knowledge/special-relativity/inertial-frames.md), [Time Dilation](knowledge/special-relativity/time-dilation.md)

**Status:** The four-velocity insight (everything moves through spacetime at exactly *c* always) is the strongest geometric support for this intuition.

**Stress-test at:** Background independence, Mach's Principle

---

## Underlying global synchronization may exist

There may be an objective "now" — a global synchronization — that SR's framework obscures because embedded observers can't access it. Simultaneity's claimed lack of ground truth may be a limitation of the measurement framework, not of reality.

**Related concepts:** [Simultaneity](knowledge/special-relativity/simultaneity.md)

**Status:** Held open. Acknowledged that SR's math works perfectly without it, and that the position is emotionally motivated (wanting an objective ground truth). Intellectual position: genuinely unresolved, not merely stubborn.

**Stress-test at:** Background independence (GR), possibly quantum mechanics
